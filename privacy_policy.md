# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** Flutter Share  
**Last updated:** 17 January 2026

Thank you for using **Flutter Share**. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Flutter Share does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or non-personal user data.**

The app is a utility application designed to **send files between devices over a local network**. All file transfers occur **directly between devices** within the same local network and **do not pass through any external servers**.

---

## 2. Permissions

Flutter Share may request certain device permissions (such as storage or network access) that are **strictly required for its core functionality**—sending and receiving files over a local network.

All permission usage is handled **locally on the user’s device**.  
**No permission-related data is collected, stored, or transmitted externally.**

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Flutter Share **does not use any third-party services**, including:

- Analytics tools  
- Advertising SDKs  
- Crash reporting services  
- Cloud storage or backend servers  

---

## 4. Data Sharing

Since Flutter Share does **not collect any data**, **no user data is shared with third parties**.

---

## 5. Data Security

As no data is collected or stored by the app, there is **no risk of data misuse, leakage, or unauthorized access** by the developer.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

Flutter Share does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
